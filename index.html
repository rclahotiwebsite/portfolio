Justice R.C. Lahoti

35th Chief Justice of India

1940 - 2022

A Life of Service & Integrity

Justice Lahoti, an eminent jurist, a great human being, and a Karma Yogi, was known for his massive contribution to the field of law and the administration of justice.

From District Court to High Court and finally to the Supreme Court, Justice Lahoti reached the pinnacle of the Indian judiciary, adorning the highest Court of the Country as Chief Justice. He made an exceptional contribution in the field of judicial reforms and upholding the constitutional rights of citizens of India. He was keenly interested in educational reforms and propagating Indian cultural values and traditions.
