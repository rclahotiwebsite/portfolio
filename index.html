Justice Lahoti , an eminent jurist, a great human being, and a Karam yogi, was known for his massive contribution to the field of law and the administration of justice and was decorated with the various honours. From District Court to High Court and finally to Supreme Court, Justice Lahoti reached the pinnacle of Indian judiciary and adorned the highest Court of the Country as Chief Justice. He made an exceptional contribution in the field of judicial reforms and upholding constitutional rights of citizens of India. He was keenly interested in educational reforms and propagating Indian cultural values and traditions.
